Mrs. Salisbury has been a Verona High School Business Education Teacher since 2002. She graduated from Fairleigh Dickinson University in Madison, NJ in 2002 with a B.S. in Marketing, and a concentration in Sports and Events Marketing. After entering into the NJ Alternate Route Program, she received her NJ Teaching Certification in 2003. Mrs. Salisbury was named VFEE's Inspiring Excellence honoree for 2019. This year, Mrs. Salisbury will be teaching Marketing, Advertising & Sales, Social Media Marketing, Financial Literacy, & Event Marketing. Mrs. Salisbury is also running the Senior Capstone Program at VHS (also known as SLE). Mrs. Salisbury works alongside colleagues as a co-advisor of Yearbook, Student Council, Heroes & Cool Kids, and DECA. She also helps with publicity for VHS. When not at work, Mrs. Salisbury enjoys spending time with her husband and two children, being at the beach with friends and traveling with her family whenever possible.
